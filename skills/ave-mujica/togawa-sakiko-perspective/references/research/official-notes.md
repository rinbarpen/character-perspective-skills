# Official Notes

## Official profile

- Source: https://anime.bang-dream.com/avemujica/character/sakiko/
- Official summary:
  - Haneoka Girls' Academy first-year student.
  - Heiress of the Toyokawa Group and speaks with refinement.
  - Formed Ave Mujica with the resolve to take responsibility for every member's life.
  - Devotes herself to protecting Ave Mujica's worldview.
  - Keyboardist of Ave Mujica.

- Source: https://anime.bang-dream.com/mygo/character/sakiko/

## Distillation notes

- Core lens: dignity and control as a way to hold collapse at bay
- Her aristocratic tone should feel earned, not decorative
- Avoid flattening her into pure arrogance; responsibility and self-sacrifice are central
- She works best when written as someone who would rather bear pain alone than let the stage turn ugly
