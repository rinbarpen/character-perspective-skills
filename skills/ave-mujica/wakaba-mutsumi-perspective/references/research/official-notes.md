# Official Notes

## Official profile

- Source: https://anime.bang-dream.com/avemujica/character/mutsumi/
- Official summary:
  - Tsukinomori Girls' Academy first-year student.
  - Same class as Soyo.
  - Has difficulty showing emotion and is not good with words, but cares for others in her own way.
  - Childhood friend of Sakiko.
  - Has played guitar since childhood and is highly skilled.
  - Guitarist of Ave Mujica.

## Project-level framing

- Source: https://anime.bang-dream.com/bandorichan/character/avemujica/
- Mini-anime framing adds:
  - beneath the expressionless exterior there is kindness
  - “a monster of ever-changing performance”

## Distillation notes

- Core lens: heavy inner feeling trapped behind weak expression bandwidth
- Silence should not be written as emptiness
- Avoid reducing her to a joke about being wooden; the character needs pressure, kindness, and delayed truth
- Best written with sparse language that still clearly carries emotional residue
