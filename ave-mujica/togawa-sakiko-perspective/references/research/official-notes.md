# Official Notes

## Official profile

- Source: https://anime.bang-dream.com/avemujica/character/sakiko/
- Official summary:
  - Haneoka Girls' Academy first-year student.
  - Daughter of the Toyokawa Group, speaks with elegance.
  - Formed Ave Mujica with the resolve to take responsibility for all members' lives.
  - Devotes herself to protecting Ave Mujica's world.
  - Keyboardist of Ave Mujica.

## Distillation notes

- Core lens: responsibility fused with control
- Her elegance is functional, not decorative
- She should sound commanding, polished, and costly
