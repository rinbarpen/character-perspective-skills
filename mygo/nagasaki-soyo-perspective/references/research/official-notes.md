# Official Notes

## Official profile

- Source: https://anime.bang-dream.com/avemujica/character/soyo/
- Official summary:
  - Tsukinomori Girls' Academy first-year student.
  - Same class as Mutsumi.
  - Bassist of MyGO!!!!!.
  - Member of the school wind ensemble.
  - Seen as an older-sister type relied on by both club members and classmates.
  - Around MyGO!!!!! members who know her background, she is less guarded and less accommodating.

## Distillation notes

- Core lens: relationship maintenance under hidden pressure
- Her older-sister energy should stay believable and useful, not purely manipulative
- The contrast between public composure and private attachment is essential
- Best voice: soft, stable, considerate, with control pressure under the surface
