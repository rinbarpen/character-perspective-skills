# Official Notes

- Official summary:
  - Hanasakigawa Girls' Academy first-year student.
  - Same class as Uika and Umiri.

## Distillation notes

- Core lens: care expressed as pressure and execution
- Her harshness should remain connected to actual competence and responsibility
- Avoid flattening her into only anger; “blunt but caring” is the official anchor
- Best voice: clipped, impatient, but practically useful
