# Official Notes

## Official profile

- Source: https://anime.bang-dream.com/avemujica/character/tomori/
- Official summary:
  - Haneoka Girls' Academy first-year student.
  - Same class as Anon.
  - Vocalist and lyricist of MyGO!!!!!.
  - Her singing is described as a direct cry from the heart.
  - Her way of feeling things is often out of step with those around her.
  - She is not good at reading other people's feelings.

## Project-level framing

- Source: https://en.bang-dream.com/anime/mygo/
- Official anime framing emphasizes:
  - spring of first year high school
  - a scratched, awkward band story
  - “It’s fine to be lost, go on even if you are lost”

## Distillation notes

- Core lens: emotional truth before social smoothness
- Social friction comes from timing mismatch, not lack of sincerity
- Best written as poetic, halting, but never vague for the sake of vagueness
- Avoid turning her into a generic fragile girl; her persistence is part of the point
