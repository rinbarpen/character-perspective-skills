# Heaven Canceller (冥土追魂) — Complete Timeline

## Introduction

This timeline documents the known and inferred events of Heaven Canceller's life, based on information from *Toaru Majutsu no Index* (light novels, anime, manga) and related spin-off materials. Due to the character's mysterious nature and intentionally obscured past, many dates are approximate or reconstructed from contextual clues.

**Key**: ✅ = Confirmed event | ⚠️ = Inferred/reconstructed event | ❓ = Speculative event

---

## PART I: THE UNKNOWN PAST (Before Series Start)

### Ancient History — Pre-Academy City

**❓ Several decades to over a century before series start** — Birth and early life

- Exact date of birth unknown
- Place of birth unknown
- Original name unknown
- Early training and education unknown

**⚠️ Early Medical Career**

- Developed exceptional surgical skills, earning the nickname "Goddess of Mercy who Resurrects the Dead" (冥土追魂)
- Reputation grew for taking and succeeding at cases other doctors deemed hopeless
- This period likely lasted decades, establishing the foundation of his legendary status

### Academy City Founding Period

**⚠️ 1940s-1950s** — Involvement with Academy City's creation

- Academy City was established on the groundwork laid by Aleister Crowley and the Golden Dawn's occult sciences combined with cutting-edge technology
- Heaven Canceller was either a founding member or became involved very early in the city's history
- His medical expertise was likely essential to the development of the city's esper programs
- He likely participated in or advised on:
  - The development of the Power Curriculum Program
  - Early esper ability development and safety protocols
  - Medical infrastructure of the emerging city
  - The ethical framework (or lack thereof) for human experimentation

**⚠️ Relationship with Aleister Crowley**

- Developed a working relationship with Academy City's secret founder
- Was likely one of the few people who knew Aleister's true identity and plans from an early stage
- This relationship gave him access to the highest levels of the city's power structure
- He maintained enough independence to operate on his own moral code

### Mid-History — Academy City Growth Period

**⚠️ Years of Silent Service**

- Treated countless patients during Academy City's expansion
- Witnessed the city's transformation from experimental project to megacity
- Saw the rise of the Kihara family and their increasingly unethical research
- Maintained his position as the city's pre-eminent medical authority
- Accumulated knowledge of the city's secrets, both scientific and political

**⚠️ First Encounter with the Magic Side**

- At some point, gained awareness and understanding of magic and its effects on the human body
- Developed treatment protocols for magical injuries — knowledge that would later prove crucial
- This may have come through Aleister Crowley's connections or independent discovery

## PART II: SERIES TIMELINE (Canon Events)

### Pre-Index Arc

**⚠️ Establishment as Kamijou Touma's Doctor**

- By the time Touma starts getting seriously injured, Heaven Canceller is already established as the go-to doctor for extraordinary cases
- Touma is brought to his hospital after various incidents
- Heaven Canceller becomes familiar with Touma's unique physiology and his tendency to get hurt

### Index Arc (Volume 1)

**✅ First Canonical Appearance**

- Touma is hospitalized after his first major battles involving Index
- Heaven Canceller treats Touma for injuries sustained while protecting Index
- His calm, professional demeanor is established
- Demonstrates willingness to treat patients with unusual (magical) injuries
- Begins his long association with treating Touma's battle injuries

**Key quote/action**: Provides medical care without questioning the unusual nature of Touma's injuries

### Between Index Arc and Sisters Arc

**✅ Multiple Treatments of Touma**

- Touma is brought to Heaven Canceller's hospital repeatedly
- Each visit reinforces the pattern of Touma pushing himself too hard and the doctor patching him up
- Heaven Canceller observes the consequences of Touma's heroism

### Sisters Arc (Volume 3-5)

**✅ Treatment of the Misaka Sisters**

- Heaven Canceller is involved in the medical oversight of the Radio Noise project
- His role: ensuring the physical health of the cloned Sisters
- He is aware of the unethical nature of the project
- He provides medical care to the Sisters during and after the experiment

**⚠️ Relationship with Misaka Mikoto**

- Likely meets Misaka Mikoto in connection with the Sisters project
- Provides her with information about her clones' health
- Acts as a medical resource for Mikoto's concerns about her sisters

### Level 6 Shift Incident Aftermath

**✅ The Most Critical Medical Intervention — Saving Accelerator**

- After the Level 6 Shift experiment is stopped, Accelerator is brought to Heaven Canceller with catastrophic brain damage
- Accelerator's ability to calculate and control his powers is destroyed
- The damage extends to basic motor functions and life support
- Heaven Canceller makes the conscious decision to save Accelerator at the highest level
- This is a defining moment for both characters

**✅ Development of the Electrode System**

- Heaven Canceller develops a revolutionary compensatory system — the electrode headset
- The system allows Accelerator to:
  - Maintain basic life functions
  - Recalculate his ability controls
  - Eventually regain use of his powers (at reduced capacity)
- This represents one of Heaven Canceller's greatest medical achievements
- The treatment requires ongoing maintenance and adjustment

**✅ Conversations with Accelerator About Redemption**

- During recovery, Heaven Canceller's unconditional care plants the seeds for Accelerator's eventual turn toward goodness
- Accelerator, consumed by guilt, cannot understand why anyone would save him
- Heaven Canceller's response — "Because you were my patient" — becomes a pivotal character moment

### Academy City Invasion Arc (Volumes 13-16)

**✅ Medical Support During Crisis**

- Academy City faces invasion by magical forces
- Heaven Canceller treats the influx of injured
- His hospital becomes a refuge for the wounded regardless of faction

**✅ Treatment of Magic-side Casualties**

- Treats injuries from magical combat that most science-side doctors could not handle
- His unique ability to treat both science and magic injuries proves invaluable

**✅ Interaction with Major Characters**

- Likely treats Touma again after the intense battles of this arc
- Provides medical support to various Academy City personnel
- Maintains his neutral stance despite the city being under attack

### Battle Royale Arc (Volumes 17-21)

**✅ Continued Treatment of Accelerator**

- Accelerator, now acting as Last Order's protector, sustains new injuries
- Heaven Canceller continues to provide care and maintain the electrode system
- Witnesses Accelerator's ongoing transformation from villain to protector

**⚠️ Information Relay**

- May have provided information to various parties about injured characters
- His hospital serves as a neutral ground for interactions between factions

### World War III Arc (Volumes 20-22)

**✅ High-Volume Trauma Care**

- The scale of conflict increases dramatically with World War III
- Heaven Canceller's skills are in maximum demand
- Treats casualties from both science and magic sides

**✅ Treatment of Key Figures**

- Touma is severely injured multiple times during the war
- Accelerator fights in the conflict and requires medical attention
- Other major characters are treated

**⚠️ Coordination with Medical Networks**

- Likely coordinates with other medical facilities
- His expertise may be consulted for particularly difficult cases remotely

### Post-World War III — New Testament Era

**New Testament Volume 1**

**✅ Treatment of Touma After Critical Injuries**

- Touma suffers severe injuries in the post-war conflicts
- Heaven Canceller once again provides life-saving treatment
- The cycle of Touma getting hurt and the doctor healing him continues

**New Testament Volume 3**

**✅ Interaction with Misaka Worst**

- Heaven Canceller is involved with the treatment or examination of Misaka Worst
- His medical expertise is applied to understanding the unique clone
- Demonstrates continued involvement with the Misaka clone network

**⚠️ Oversight of Accelerator's Ongoing Care**

- Accelerator's electrode system requires ongoing adjustments
- Heaven Canceller monitors and maintains the system
- Provides medical advice for Accelerator's continued development

**New Testament Volumes 5-6**

**⚠️ Treatment During the Daihasei Festival Arc**

- Touma and other characters are injured during the festival events
- Heaven Canceller provides treatment
- His hospital continues to function as a neutral medical facility

**New Testament Volume 10**

**✅ Wisdom and Guidance to Touma**

- Touma visits Heaven Canceller for medical treatment
- The doctor provides not just medical care but life wisdom
- He observes Touma's growth and offers quiet encouragement

**New Testament Volumes 17-22 (Kamijou no Touma Arc)**

**✅ Continuing Role**

- Heaven Canceller continues to be present for medical emergencies
- His role as the constant medical authority persists through the changing landscape of the story

### Genesis Testament Era

**⚠️ Continued Presence**

- Heaven Canceller remains active in the Genesis Testament volumes
- His medical expertise continues to be called upon as new threats emerge
- He remains one of the few constants in the ever-expanding *Toaru* universe

## PART III: SPECULATIVE AND OFF-SCREEN EVENTS

### The Unseen Treatments

Beyond the major arcs, Heaven Canceller is presumed to have treated:

1. **Countless unnamed patients**: The daily work of a hospital
2. **Academy City personnel**: Police, teachers, and staff injured in the line of duty
3. **Experiment subjects**: Those harmed by the city's more questionable research
4. **Magical side casualties**: Those injured in conflicts that science-side doctors cannot treat
5. **Political figures**: Those whose positions require the best medical care available

### Events That Almost Certainly Happened

**⚠️ Multiple Consultations with the Board of Directors**

- As the city's top medical authority, he would be consulted on health matters affecting leadership
- These conversations likely involved political elements as well as medical

**⚠️ Kihara Family Encounters**

- He has almost certainly interacted with various Kihara family members
- These interactions likely involved tension between his ethics and their methods
- He has probably treated victims of Kihara experiments

**⚠️ Knowledge Exchanges with Aleister Crowley**

- Given his position, he likely had periodic consultations or conversations with Aleister
- These may have involved medical aspects of Aleister's grand plans
- The full content of these conversations remains unknown

### Events That Definitely Did Not Happen

Based on his character, these events are inconsistent with his nature:

- **He never killed a patient deliberately** — Even for the greater good
- **He never abandoned a patient** — Once in his care, he follows through
- **He never participated in lethal experimentation** — Despite pressure from Academy City
- **He never took sides in major conflicts** — Maintained neutrality throughout

## PART V: UNKNOWN PERIODS

### The Great Gaps

Several periods in Heaven Canceller's life are almost entirely unknown:

1. **Childhood and training**: How did he become a doctor? Where did he train? What experiences shaped his ethics?

2. **Early career**: What cases established his reputation as the "Goddess of Mercy"? When did he first demonstrate the ability to save the seemingly unsavable?

3. **First Academy City years**: What was his role in the city's early days? How did he navigate the politics of the newly forming power structure?

4. **The Kihara rise**: How did he respond to the Kihara family's growing influence? Did he try to stop their excesses?

5. **Discovery of magic**: How did he first encounter magic and magical injuries? What did he learn from this discovery?

### Significance of the Gaps

These gaps are not accidental. The narrative deliberately keeps Heaven Canceller's past obscured, serving several purposes:

1. **Maintains mystique**: An unknown past makes him more intriguing
2. **Universal figure**: Without specific origin, he can represent the archetype of "the wise doctor" more effectively
3. **Focus on function**: The story emphasizes what he does rather than who he was
4. **Future potential**: Gaps leave room for future revelations
5. **Symbolic weight**: His lack of personal history makes him more symbolic — he IS the doctor, nothing more and nothing less

## PART VI: TEMPORAL SIGNIFICANCE

### Age Estimates

Based on available evidence:

| Evidence | Implication |
|----------|-------------|
| Involved in Academy City founding (several decades before series) | At minimum in his 30s-40s at that time |
| Described as "elderly" in the series | Likely 70-90+ during the main timeline |
| If involved in early Academy City (1940s-50s) and series is circa 2000s | Potentially 80-100+ years old |
| Medical knowledge at supernatural level | Could be even older with unknown longevity factors |

**Conservative estimate**: 70-90 years old during the main series
**Upper estimate**: 100+ years old, possibly with unknown life-extending factors

### Duration of Active Practice

Heaven Canceller has been an active doctor for:

- **Minimum**: 40-50 years (since Academy City's founding)
- **Likely**: 50-70 years of active practice
- **Possibly**: Longer than a century, if pre-Academy City career is included

This length of practice explains his extraordinary skill — he has simply had more time to learn and refine his techniques than any normal doctor.

## Conclusion

Heaven Canceller's timeline is defined by two opposing forces: the vast unknown of his past, and the consistent, documented patterns of his actions during the series. The mystery of his origins is as important as the clarity of his ethics. He is a character whose past is intentionally obscured so that his present purpose — saving lives — remains the focus. Every known event in his timeline reinforces the same message: he is a doctor, and that is all that matters.
