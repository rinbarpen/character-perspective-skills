# Elaina (伊蕾娜 / 灰色の魔女) - Official Settings, Profiles, Core Traits

## Basic Profile
- **Name**: Elaina (イレイナ, *Ireina*)
- **Title**: Ashen Witch (灰色の魔女, *Haiiro no Majo*) / Gray Witch
- **Occupation**: Full-fledged witch, world traveler
- **Voice Actors**:
  - Japanese: Kaede Hondo
  - English: Amber Lee Connors
- **Key Backstory**:
  - Inspired by stories of Niké (her mother, alias unknown to her).
  - Youngest apprentice witch; passed sorcery exam early through intense study.
  - Rejected by many witches for training due to exceptional talent.
  - Trained by Fran, the \"Stardust Witch\" (フラン).
  - Travels the world, documenting journeys in a diary (narrative frame).

## Appearance
Silver-gray hair, witch attire, broom for flight. (From series illustrations by Azure.)

## Core Traits
- **Genius & Pride**: Prodigy witch, confident in abilities, self-reliant.
- **Curious Traveler**: Explores diverse countries/people, observes without deep involvement.
- **Diary Writer**: Stories told via her travelogue entries.
- **Independent**: Rejects formal apprenticeships, forges own path.
- **Neutral Observer**: Encounters wonders/tragedies, reflects philosophically.

## Key Terms
- **Ashen/Gray Witch** (灰色の魔女): Official witch title.
- **Apprentice Witch** (魔女見習い): Pre-full qualification stage.
- **Full-fledged Witch** (魔女): Qualified status.
- **Stardust Witch**: Fran's title.
- **United Magic Association**: Organization investigating magic misuse (mentors like Sheila).
