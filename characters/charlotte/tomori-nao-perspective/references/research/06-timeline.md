# 06 Timeline: Nao Tomori - Character Arc, Growth, Key Events

## Overview
Nao Tomori's arc in *Charlotte* (anime, manga adaptations, OVA) transitions from an isolated, distrustful leader hardened by trauma to a vulnerable partner who embodies hope and commitment. Her growth centers on gradually opening her emotional barriers, particularly through her relationship with Yuu Otosaka.

## Detailed Timeline

### Pre-Story (Childhood/Background)
- **Ability Manifestation**: Develops selective invisibility (invisible to one person at a time). Uses it aggressively to "kick" bullies, leading to isolation and no female friends. 
- **Family Trauma**: Brother Kazuki Tomori (air/vibration control for guitar) experimented on, resulting in insanity/vegetative state. Nao escapes with him to safety, fueling her mission to protect ability users.
- **Growth Marker**: Builds emotional walls, becomes shrewd, self-righteous, but deeply committed to justice.
  - Credibility: High (Ep2, Fandom Wiki)

### Main Story (Anime Episodes 1-13, 2015)
1. **Ep1: Recruitment (July 5, 2015 air)**: Exposes Yuu Otosaka's possession ability, beats him invisibly, forces transfer to Hoshinoumi Academy student council. Establishes her as authoritative leader.
   
2. **Ep2: Vulnerability (July 12)**: Reveals brother's fate to Yuu, shows rare openness. Explains student council's protective role.

3. **Ep3-4: Team Building**: Leads missions vs. thoughtography user (Ep2, but ongoing), recruits Yusa Nishimori/Misa. Demonstrates strategic planning (baseball match Ep4).

4. **Ep5-6: Bonding & Crisis**: Camps for flight user (Ep5). Supports Yuu during Ayumi's collapse incident (Ep6); first signs of care beyond duty.

5. **Ep7: Emotional Anchor**: Pulls depressed Yuu from reclusion with omurice (Ayumi's recipe). Pivotal growth: shifts from manipulator to empathetic supporter.

6. **Ep8: Deepening Romance**: Invites Yuu to Zhiend concert (shared interest). Nostalgic bonding; admits feelings indirectly.

7. **Ep9-10: Truth Revealed**: Learns Yuu's full plunder ability & brother Shunsuke. Supports time-leap to save Ayumi.

8. **Ep11: Sacrifice & Loss**: Kidnapped by terrorists with Kumagami; he dies shielding her. Yuu loses eye rescuing. Heightens stakes, solidifies bond.

9. **Ep12: Promise**: Confesses love, offers ability for normal life. Yuu plunders it; vows to wait.

10. **Ep13: Resolution (Sep 27)**: With amnesiac Yuu, declares "we're lovers." Embodies unwavering commitment; arc completes as pillar of new normalcy.

### Spin-offs & Adaptations
- **OVA: "Tsuyoi Monotachi" (Mar 30, 2016, between Ep4-5)**: Helps mind-reading girl Iori Sekiguchi at amusement park despite thoughts exposed. Shows empathy for burdened youth; comedic yet reinforces protective nature.
- **Manga (Dengeki G's Comic, 2015-2019)**: Faithful adaptation; no major divergences in arc.
- **4-koma Manga (2015-2017)**: Humorous side-stories; highlights tsundere traits.
- **No Recent Spin-offs**: Last content 2016-2019; no new as of 2026.
