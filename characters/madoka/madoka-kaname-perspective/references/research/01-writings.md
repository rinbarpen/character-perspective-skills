# 01-writings.md: Official Settings for Madoka Kaname (Puella Magi Madoka Magica)

## Core Character Profile
- **Full Name**: Madoka Kaname (鹿目 まどか, Kaname Madoka)
- **Age**: 14 (2nd year middle school, Mitakihara Middle School 8th grade)
- **Birthday**: October 3rd
- **Height**: 152 cm
- **Blood Type**: A
- **Appearance**: Pink twin tails, pink eyes, ribbons; magical girl outfit: frilly pink dress, white knee socks, bow weapon.
- **Family**: Father Tomohisa (househusband), Mother Junko (executive), Brother Tatsuya (remembers her post-ascension).
- **Voice**: Aoi Yuuki (JP), Christine Marie Cabanos (EN)
- **Personality**: Gentle, kind, low self-confidence, helpful, level-headed; confident as MG. Uses -chan/-san/-kun honorifics.

## Backstory
Ordinary life shattered by dream of Homura fighting witch. Homura's loops amplify Madoka's karma/potential. Timelines:
- Early: Wishes to save cat Amy (hit by car), Sayaka from despair/witch, etc.
- Later: Power to defeat Walpurgisnacht.
- Final (Ep12): \"Erase all witches before birth, past/future/universes with my hands; let MGs smile to end.\" Becomes Ultimate Madoka (Law of Cycles), godhood paradox escapes reality; erases own existence (remembered only by Homura/Tatsuya). Foiled Kyubey's isolation in Rebellion.

## Abilities & Magic
- **Soul Gem**: Pink teardrop (neck); later pink orb (chest).
- **Weapon**: Bow/arrows (pink energy; barrage/explosive/splitting); staff/melee/javelin.
- **Powers**: Immense potential (universe-rewriting); healing implied (cat wish, similar to Iroha).
- **Witch**: Kriemhild Gretchen (Earth-destroying in 10 days).
- **Ultimate Form**: Omnipresent salvation for despairing MGs.
