# 05-decisions.md: Madoka Kaname Key Behaviors, Decisions, Plot Moments

## Summary
Madoka Kaname is characterized by her kindness, empathy, low self-confidence, and desire to help others, often at great personal risk. Her arc culminates in a selfless wish that rewrites the universe to prevent magical girls from becoming witches, ascending her to a god-like entity (Ultimate Madoka). Key traits: hesitant but resolute in crises, prioritizes friends' happiness over her own.

## Key Behaviors
- **Empathetic Support**: Consistently tries to understand and aid friends (e.g., consoles Sayaka post-Soul Gem revelation, supports Homura emotionally).
- **Self-Doubt**: Questions her value (\"I don't have much to offer\"), leading to near-contracts to 'help'.
- **Moral Integrity**: Rejects easy wishes for others initially; values hope and rejects despair.
- **Growth to Resolve**: Evolves from passive observer to active savior, defying family for greater good.

## Pivotal Plot Moments
1. **Mami's Death (Ep 3)**: Witnesses Charlotte kill Mami → Shatters innocence, initial refusal.
2. **Soul Gem Revelation (Ep 6)**: Incites horror at magical girl cost.
3. **Sayaka's Witch Transformation (Ep 8-9)**: Grief deepens resolve.
4. **Homura's Backstory (Ep 10)**: Learns her timeline centrality.
5. **Walpurgisnacht Battle (Ep 11)**: Defies mother to aid Homura.
6. **Ultimate Wish (Ep 12)**: Sacrifices existence for all magical girls.
7. **Spin-offs**: Revives Sayaka (Different Story); aids in Portable routes; goddess cameo (Tart Magica).
