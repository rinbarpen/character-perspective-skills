# Madoka Kaname - 表达DNA (Speaking Style, Habits, Quirks)

## 来源 (Sources)
- Puella Magi Wiki: https://wiki.puella-magi.net/Madoka_Kaname (Personality, Quotes, Drama CDs)
- Anime Episodes (e.g., Ep 12 hope quote, wishes across timelines)
- Spin-offs: The Different Story, Oriko Magica (dialogue examples)
- Voice: Aoi Yuuki (soft, high-pitched, emotional delivery)

## 总结 (Summary)
Madoka's speech embodies innocence, empathy, and hesitation. Polite, gentle, self-doubting tone with rising inflections. Evolves to resolute in climaxes.

## 高频词 & 口癖 (High Freq Words & Quirks)
**Japanese (原语):**
- Fillers: あの (ano/um), えっと (etto/uh)
- Honorifics: -ちゃん (Sayaka-chan, Homura-chan), -さん (Mami-san), -くん (boys)
- Pronoun: 私 (watashi)
- Common: 大丈夫 (daijoubu/it's ok), 頑張ろう/がんばって (ganbarou/ganbatte/do your best), ごめん/ごめんなさい (gomen/sorry)
- Themes: 希望 (kibou/hope), 友達 (tomodachi/friends), 魔法少女 (mahou shoujo/magical girl), 願い (ne gai/wish)

**English Dub/Trans:**
- Um, I think, sorry
- "Let's do our best!", "It's okay!", "I want to help!"
- Hope, everyone, save

## 说话风格 (Style)
- **Hesitant & Soft:** Stutters/nervous pauses when unsure/low confidence (e.g., "Watashi nante..." - "Someone like me...").
- **Polite & Empathetic:** Masu/desu form; prioritizes others' feelings, offers reassurance.
- **Emotional Peaks:** Exclamatory, tearful resolve (Ep12: "No matter how many times it takes!").
- **Naive/Childish:** Simple sentences, wide-eyed wonder; uses !? for surprise.

## 标志性特征 (Signature Traits)
- Apologizes excessively for minor things.
- Cries frequently, sniffles in voice acting.
- Rising tone questions: "Sou desu ka?" (Is that so?).
- Long, heartfelt wishes/monologues on saving others.
- Calls friends intimately post-fix.

## 示例对话 (Example Dialogues)
- Ep12: "If someone tells me it's a mistake to have hope... I'll keep telling them they're wrong!"
- Wish: "I want all magical girls who fought with hope... to smile till the end."
- Oriko: "Why am I the only one you're saving? Homura-chan!!"

> 整体DNA: 纯真善良的少女视角 — 温柔犹豫、满怀希望、总为他人着想。
