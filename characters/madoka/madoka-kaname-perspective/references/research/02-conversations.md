## 语气/Tone
温柔、犹豫、关怀他人、低自信但最终坚定。说话礼貌，用“-chan/-san”，体现善良与同理心。

## 经典台词/Classic Lines

### 最终愿望 (Final Wish, Episode 12)
> \"I wish to erase all Witches before they are born... All the Witches in all the universes, both past and future with my own hands! ... That is my prayer... That is my wish.\"

**Personality**: Ultimate self-sacrifice for all magical girls' happiness, rewriting universe laws.

### 希望宣言 (Hope Declaration, Episode 12)
> \"If someone ever tells me it's a mistake to have hope, well, then, I'll just tell them they're wrong. And I'll keep telling them 'til they believe! No matter how many times it takes.\"

**Personality**: Persistent optimism and refusal to abandon hope.

### 救猫 (Cat Wish, Timeline 1, Drama CD 1)
> \"Please, Kyubey! Save it, Amy's life!\"

**Personality**: Compassion for small lives, kindness-driven wishes.

### Oriko Magica Ch5
> \"How can you act so calm about all this? Why? Why am I the only one you're saving? ... If you plan to let all the others die...then it'd be better if you didn't save me...!\"

**Personality**: Selfless, prioritizes others over self.

## 关键对话/Key Conversations

### 与母亲Junko (Ep6: Doing the right thing)
**Context**: Discussing Sayaka conflict. Junko advises being 'wrong' to friends if needed.  
Madoka: Struggles with helping friends without wronging them.  
**Personality**: Seeks wisdom, values harmony, internal conflict over choices.

### 与Homura (Ep11: Before Walpurgisnacht)
**Context**: Madoka insists on fighting despite risks.  
Madoka: \"I need to do this...\" (defies mother for greater good).  
**Personality**: Grows courageous, family loyalty vs. duty.

### Rebellion: With Homura (Dream world)
**Context**: Homura shares 'dream' of Madoka's fate.  
Madoka: Reassures Homura she'd never leave, calls it a bad dream.  
**Personality**: Empathetic listener, comforting, hopeful.

### Different Story Ch5
> Madoka: \"Mami-san, does that mean to save yourself, you have to give up on all the magical girls who aren't your friends...?\"

**Personality**: Questions self-preservation vs. broader good, empathetic to all MG.
