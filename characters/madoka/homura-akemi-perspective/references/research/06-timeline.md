# Homura Akemi Timeline & Character Arc\n\n## Summary\nHomura Akemi begins as a frail, shy hospital patient bullied for her weakness. Befriended by Madoka, she witnesses tragedy during Walpurgisnacht, contracts as a magical girl, and loops time ~100 times to protect Madoka. Each failure hardens her into a cold, tactical fighter obsessed with her salvation. Post-Madoka's ascension, she fights wraiths alone. In Rebellion, despair turns her into witch Homulilly; rejecting fate, she becomes Akuma Homura—a demon embodying love—rewriting the universe to give Madoka a normal life while holding her divine self captive.\n\n## Key Events\n\n### Pre-Contract (Timeline 1)\n- Hospitalized with heart disease; wears glasses, braids; bullied, low self-esteem.\n- Transfers to Mitakihara Middle School (March/May 25).\n- Saved by Madoka & Mami from witch Izabel.\n- Walpurgisnacht destroys city; Madoka sacrifices self. Homura wishes: \"Meet Madoka again, strong enough to protect her.\"\n\n### Loops (Timelines 2-100)\n- Gains time-stop/rewind shield; heals self, ditches glasses/braids.\n- Steals weapons, hones skills, predicts events statistically.\n- Fails repeatedly: Friends die/witchify (Mami by Charlotte, Sayaka→Oktavia, Kyoko suicides, Madoka→Kriemhild Gretchen).\n- Grows distant, cold; single-minded focus on preventing Madoka's contract.\n\n### Main Timeline (Timeline ~100)\n- Transfers as cool transfer student; warns Madoka.\n- Timeline events: Mami dies, Sayaka witches, Kyoko dies vs. Oktavia.\n- Fights Walpurgisnacht alone; Madoka ascends as Law of Cycles, erases witches.\n\n### Post-Ascension (Wraith Arc)\n- Retains memories; fights wraiths with bow, memory magic.\n- Ribbon from Madoka as keepsake.\n\n### Rebellion\n- Isolated by Incubators; subconscious witch barrier (Homulilly).\n- Awakens as witch; saved by Madoka.\n- Rejects godhood; shatters Soul Gem, becomes Akuma Homura.\n- Rewrites universe: Madoka human, friends happy; Homura as \"demon\" overseeing.\n\n## Arc & Growth\n- **Timid Victim → Protector**: From helplessness to hyper-competence.\n- **Innocence → Obsession**: Love twists into yandere fixation; trauma builds emotional walls.\n- **Human → Demon**: Ultimate sacrifice—bears world's sins to grant Madoka selfish wish.\n\n## Sources\n- Puella Magi Wiki: [Homura Akemi](https://wiki.puella-magi.net/Homura_Akemi), [Timeline](https://wiki.puella-magi.net/Timeline)\n- Full research from official anime, Rebellion, Wraith Arc, manga spin-offs (Oriko, Different Story).
