# External Views: Fan Opinions and CP (Ships) for Homura Akemi

## Summary
Homura Akemi is one of the most beloved and complex characters in *Puella Magi Madoka Magica*. Fans adore her for her profound devotion to Madoka Kaname, her tragic backstory involving endless time loops, stoic demeanor, and evolution into a powerful anti-heroine. She consistently ranks at the top of popularity polls (e.g., #1 in multiple Newtype and NHK polls). Criticisms focus on her yandere traits, obsessiveness, and morally ambiguous actions in *Rebellion*. 

CP (shipping): The overwhelmingly dominant pairing is **HomuMado** (Homura/Madoka), interpreted as romantic by much of the fandom due to key scenes; it's a cornerstone of Madoka yuri culture with massive fanworks. Minor ships include Homura/Kyoko.

## Fan Opinions
- **Praise**: Ultimate loyalty, badass design, emotional depth. &quot;Homura's love transcends time—pure inspiration.&quot; (Common on Reddit/Twitter).
- **Critique**: Possessive stalker, Rebellion twist divisive (&quot;villainized Madoka&quot; vs. &quot;necessary sacrifice&quot;).
- Popularity metrics: ~170k MAL favorites; top seller in figures/merch; frequent #1 in anime character polls.

## CP Discussions
- **HomuMado**: Semi-canon (confession in Rebellion). 12k+ AO3 fics, 500k+ Pixiv illustrations under combined tags. Fan debates: platonic sisterhood vs. explicit romance.
- **Others**: HomuKyouko (rivalry ship, niche); Homura self-ship or gen.
- Fandom consensus: Homura's arc fuels queer readings, boosting yuri appeal.
