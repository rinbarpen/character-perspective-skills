# 02-conversations.md\n\n## 美树沙耶加经典台词与对话\n\n### 主要经典台词（英文原版及大致翻译）\n\n1. **Episode 4**: \"Hey, Mami. See that? My wish’s been granted. There’s no way I’ll ever regret it. And right now, this is the happiest moment of my life.\"\n   - 嘿，麻美。看到了吗？我的愿望实现了。我绝不会后悔。现在是我人生中最幸福的时刻。\n\n2. **Episode 12 (Farewell)**: \"I'm okay with that... All I ever really wanted was to hear him play again... Just remembering that again is enough for me. I don't have any regrets anymore.\"\n   - 我没关系……我真正想要的只是再次听到他拉小提琴……回忆起那就足够了。我不再有遗憾了。\n\n3. **To Kyoko**: \"Don't talk to me like you know everything! I fight for justice!\"\n   - 不要像什么都懂的样子教训我！我是为了正义而战！\n\n4. **On becoming magical girl**: \"With this power, I can heal anyone! I'm a real magical girl now!\"\n   - 用这力量，我能治愈任何人！我现在是真正的魔法少女了！\n\n5. **Despair**: \"I'm just a monster now... No different from the witches...\"\n   - 我现在只是个怪物……和魔女没什么两样……\n\n### 关键对话片段\n\n- **与Madoka (early)**: Sayaka excitedly shares her wish to heal Kyosuke's hand, embodying her heroic ideals.\n\n- **Conflict with Kyoko**: Sayaka rejects Kyoko's pragmatic view, insisting on her sense of justice over survival.\n\n- **Final moments**: Accepts her fate peacefully, happy for Kyosuke's happiness with Hitomi.\n\n**来源**: Puella Magi Wiki, anime episodes. 更多台词可扩展自剧集字幕。
