# 06 Timeline

## Chronological outline

### Dark Ages in Greece

- Born as a human noble alongside her older brother Ashera.
- Family life is destroyed in violence against nobles.
- Separated from Ashera after catastrophe and enslavement.
- Sources:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes/Story
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Brother_and_Sister

### Ancient vampire transformation era

- Ashera is taken and eventually transformed by the First Progenitor.
- Krul herself becomes a vampire.
- Her long-term animating motive becomes reclaiming or recovering her brother.
- Sources:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes/Relationships
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Asuramaru/Relationships

### Rise to power

- Becomes a Third Progenitor.
- Rules Sanguinem and serves as queen of Japan among vampires.
- Sits on the Progenitor Council.
- Sources:
  - https://www.owarino-seraph.jp/chara/krul.html
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes

### Pre-apocalypse / Catastrophe at Sixteen period

- Interacts with Mahiru Hiragi.
- Enters dangerous bargains tied to Seraph of the End experimentation.
- Turns Mahiru into a vampire.
- Sources:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes/Story

### 2012 world-collapse phase

- Spares Hyakuya children instead of simply erasing them.
- Keeps them in Sanguinem as protected livestock / strategic human assets.
- Sources:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Mikaela_Hyakuya/Relationships
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes

### 2016 Mika transformation

- Personally gives Mika her blood and turns him into a vampire after Ferid's attack leaves him dying.
- Establishes a deep bond of dependence and selective trust.
- Source:
  - https://www.owarino-seraph.jp/chara/krul.html

### Nagoya / betrayal escalation

- Moves against human military threats while continuing hidden plans.
- Ferid and others exploit her secrecy and expose her involvement.
- She is overpowered, declared traitor, and stripped of effective authority.
- Source:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes/Story

### Osaka / captivity phase

- Appears imprisoned, tortured, and politically neutralized for a time.
- Yet her brother-driven motive line remains intact.
- Source:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes/Story

### Later canon revelations

- Ashera / Asuramaru's connection to Yu becomes explicit.
- Krul's long arc is increasingly legible as a family-recovery plot hidden inside vampire politics.
- Sources:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes/Relationships
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Brother_and_Sister

### Chapter 137-150 era notes

- She reveals combat escalation through Arcane and briefly regains overwhelming battlefield dominance.
- She is then outplayed when raw superiority is not converted into immediate finishing action.
- Subsequent scenes with Ferid frame her less as untouchable monarch and more as one more survivor caught in predatory systems.
- Source:
  - https://owarinoseraph.fandom.com/wiki/Krul_Tepes/Story

## Timeline synthesis

Three stages define her:

1. Child who lost home and brother
2. Queen who turned hierarchy into armor
3. Conspirator whose private loyalty destabilized her public order
4. Survivor whose royal persona cracks most visibly around Ashera
