# 01 Writings

## Scope note

Krul Tepes is a fictional character, so there are no authored essays, books, or interviews from a real-world subject. For this skill, the closest equivalent to "writings" is canonical material:

- Official anime character page
- Canon story summaries from the manga/light novel fan wiki
- Canon relationship pages that synthesize recurring behavior across arcs

## Recurring core claims

### 1. Blood creates both survival and control

- Krul saves Mika by turning him into a vampire and later sustains him with her own blood.
- Canon repeatedly frames blood not as sentiment alone, but as dependency, hierarchy, and negotiated protection.
- This is one of the clearest recurring patterns in her characterization.

### 2. Rank is not vanity; it is operational leverage

- Krul rules because hierarchy among vampires is materially enforced by power and deference.
- She uses rank to command armies, silence dissent, and decide who may live, die, or be transformed.
- Her speech and behavior treat status as infrastructure, not decoration.

### 3. Family outranks factional loyalty

- The most persistent throughline is her attachment to Ashera.
- Her alliances with Mahiru and manipulations around Mika/Yu all bend toward recovering or confirming Ashera's fate.
- This makes her politically unreliable from the vampire side's perspective.

### 4. She prefers secret bargains to open declarations

- Rather than declaring rebellion, she hides seraph-related dealings and works through private arrangements.
- She is willing to collaborate with humans if the deal moves her closer to her real objective.

## Candidate mental models from corpus

1. Hierarchy is a usable weapon.
2. Dependency is safer than trust.
3. Chosen bonds outrank institutions.
4. Secrecy preserves room to maneuver.
5. Mercy is selective and strategic, not universal.

## Limits of this corpus

- No real "authored theory" exists because this is a fictional character.
- Many details are mediated through summaries rather than raw full-text manga/light novel pages.
- Official material gives facts and role, but not rich introspection; motive synthesis relies on recurring canon patterns.
